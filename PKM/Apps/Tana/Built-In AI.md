## Q2:

How do you use AI chat on really large knowledge graphs? For instance, imagine if I ported over all of my notes from other apps. I’d want to centralise all of this into one large living wiki. It would give me personalised answers to my life and everything I encounter inside it. In Notion, the AI is able to use semantic search to retrieve answers. It seems to do this effortlessly and there’s no concept of tokens.  
> 如何在超大型知识图谱上使用 AI 聊天？例如，假设我把其他应用里的所有笔记都导入到 Notion 中。我希望把所有这些内容集中到一个大型的动态维基中。它能为我提供关于生活以及我遇到的所有事情的个性化答案。在 Notion 中，AI 能够使用语义搜索来检索答案。它似乎毫不费力地就做到了这一点，而且没有词元（token）的概念。
 
In Tana, it seems like you need to push the whole thing to an AI model. What’s the point in having built-in AI if you can barely use it to search your graph? Maybe I’m wrong here, but most people have a separate AI subscription as well as an app like Tana. What are the best use cases for Tana AI? Aside from auto filling fields or custom command commands.  
> 在 Tana 中，似乎需要将所有操作都交给 AI 模型处理。如果内置 AI 几乎无法用于搜索图谱，那它存在的意义何在？也许我理解有误，但大多数人除了 Tana 这类应用外，还会单独订阅 AI 服务。除了自动填充字段或自定义命令之外，Tana AI 的最佳应用场景是什么？
