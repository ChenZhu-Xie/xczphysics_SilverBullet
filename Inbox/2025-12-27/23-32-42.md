## Message 1

I do sometimes look at my now insanely-complex-[[PKM/Apps/Tana]]-graph-(-by- comparison-to-my-Workflowy-graph) and ask myself if I'm actually better off.  
> 我有时会看着我现在极其复杂的 Tana 图（与我的 Workflowy 图相比），然后问自己，我是否真的变得更好了。

Yes, my data is now dutifully structured, but I can't get it outside of Tana to do anything with it. In Airtable or other database apps, that integrate natively with n8n, Make, Zapier, etc. it functionally makes total sense to structure data this way. But with Tana, it's just stuck sitting in there. Workflowy introduced a proper API this year and people are already doing some really neat things with it.  
> 是的，我的数据现在已经按规范组织好了，但我无法将其从 Tana 中导出进行任何操作。在 Airtable 或其他与 n8n、Make、Zapier 等原生集成的数据库应用中，这种数据结构完全合理。但在 Tana 中，数据却只能束之高阁。Workflowy 今年推出了完善的 API，人们已经用它做出了许多非常棒的东西。

Workflowy also has a fully functioning and delightful mobile app that lets me browse and interact with entire graph.  
> Workflowy 还拥有一个功能齐全且令人愉悦的移动应用程序，让我可以浏览整个图表并与之互动。

At least my personal Tana graph is ultimately just a tree hierarchy of well organized search nodes, and while it's great that they organize themselves fluidly, I still can't — after 3 years — just instantly search my tree and see where my notes live in context to the tree (like I can do in Workflowy). I grew up with Google. I just want to search. Especially on mobile. I can't tell you how easy it used to be to open my Workflowy app and search for a person's name and find all 20 recent things related to that person, walk into a meeting, and feel prepared. With Tana on mobile, it's a mess. I can quickly write a thought down, but that's about all I can use it for. 
> 至少我的个人 Tana 图最终只是一个由组织良好的搜索节点构成的树状层级结构。虽然它们能够流畅地自我组织这一点很棒，但三年过去了，我仍然无法像在 Workflowy 中那样，瞬间搜索我的树状结构，并查看我的笔记在树状结构中的位置。我从小就用 Google，我只想搜索，尤其是在移动设备上。我无法形容以前打开 Workflowy 应用，搜索一个人的名字，就能找到与此人相关的 20 条最新动态，然后走进会议室，感觉一切准备就绪是多么轻松。而 Tana 的移动版简直一团糟。我可以快速记下一些想法，但也仅此而已。

But Workflowy is “just” a fancy text editor, that's it. Your entire graph is housed in a single plaintext file you can export whenever you want. Tana is an interconnected database application, complete with relationships, fields, and more. That's great and all, but most of the time I just want the scratchpad of my daily page, which Workflowy finally features natively (but is a mechanic I had already used for a decade with the fabulous WFx plugin).  
> 但 Workflowy “仅仅”是一个功能强大的文本编辑器，仅此而已。你的整个图表都保存在一个纯文本文件中，你可以随时导出。Tana 是一个互联的数据库应用程序，包含关系、字段等等。这固然很棒，但大多数时候，我只需要记录每日工作内容的草稿本，而 Workflowy 最终也原生支持这个功能（不过，我十年前就已经通过出色的 WFx 插件实现了类似的功能）。

But every time I left Workflowy over the years for another app I came back to Workflowy. Until Tana. Tana does feel like an evolution of Workflowy. The product the Workflowy team never built but could have. Ultimately though, their brand is about the power of simplicity. And at that, they excel greatly.  
这些年来，每次我离开 Workflowy 去用其他应用，最终都会回到 Workflowy。直到 Tana 的出现。Tana 的确感觉像是 Workflowy 的进化版，是 Workflowy 团队从未开发过，但本可以开发出来的产品。归根结底，他们的品牌理念是“简洁的力量”。而在这方面，他们做得非常出色。

Le sigh. We're spoiled for choice in this niche these days.  
> 唉，如今这个细分领域的选择真是太多了。

## Message 2

Ah yes, I did actually really like OmniOutliner. They were a purely native app with a matching iOS app, and you could do location-based reminders with it like 20 years ago. It was awesome. When I'm near the grocery store, here's my grocery list. It's insane that nobody else has cracked that as well as they did so long ago. Ultimately their interface was too opinionated for me.  
> 啊，没错，我确实很喜欢 OmniOutliner。它是一款纯原生应用，还有配套的 iOS 应用，而且早在 20 年前就能实现基于位置的提醒功能。真是太棒了！比如，当我靠近超市时，我的购物清单就会自动显示。难以置信的是，这么久以来竟然没有人能像他们那样把这个功能做得这么好。不过，最终对我来说，他们的界面还是太过于个性化了。

[2:04](https://tanacommunity.slack.com/archives/C04K0V87F99/p1765303490000179?thread_ts=1765302104.541289&cid=C04K0V87F99)

Yes, other outliners existed, but Workflowy popularized it conceptually, IMHO. They turned it into a humble art form. Shots fired at *the page*. Why limit yourself in scope when you can simply... zoom... zoom... zoom.  
> 是的，以前也有其他大纲工具，但 Workflowy 在概念上真正普及了它，恕我直言。他们把它变成了一种朴素的艺术形式。就像在*页面*上开火一样。既然可以简单地……放大……放大……放大，为什么要限制自己的视野呢？

## Message 3

The other thing Workflowy still has over Tana is it is obsessively keyboard focused. You do not need a mouse for *anything* in Workflowy. Every command, every navigation mechanic, all keyboard friendly. Nothing is no more than 3 keystrokes away.  
> Workflowy 比 Tana 更胜一筹的地方在于它极其注重键盘操作。在 Workflowy 中，你完全不需要*鼠标* 。所有命令、所有导航机制都支持键盘操作。任何操作都只需按三下键即可完成。

## Message 4

Let's see... in the past few months they've released:  
我们来看看……在过去的几个月里，他们发布了：

*   proper markdown pasting (and even easier markdown export)  
>    正确的 Markdown 粘贴（以及更便捷的 Markdown 导出）
*   list sorting natively without WFx  
>    无需 WFx 即可原生实现列表排序
*   chat with your notes (AI)  
>    与笔记（人工智能）聊天
*   add content from template (similar to supertags, templatized content, way more flexible since its just text but not truly structured)  
>    从模板添加内容（类似于超级标签、模板化内容，由于只是文本但并非真正结构化，因此更加灵活）
*   login with google  使用谷歌账号登录
*   native dividers  原生分隔符
*   color and highlight multiple nodes at once  
>    同时对多个节点进行着色和高亮显示
*   date auto-complete improvements, search by links as a query type 
>    日期自动完成功能改进，支持按链接搜索作为查询类型
*   global desktop quick add across all apps (how many times have we asked for that?)  
>    全局桌面快速添加功能（适用于所有应用程序）（我们已经要求过多少次了？）
*   many more slash commands for quick access to stuff  
>    更多斜杠命令，方便快速访问各种内容
*   native shortcuts for any note, (jump to your grocery list by just typing "gl" in search)  
>    任何笔记的本地快捷键（只需在搜索框中输入“gl”即可跳转到您的购物清单）
*   OCR in images within workflowy  
>    工作流程中的图像 OCR
*   readwise sync  读取同步
*   advanced calendar integrations  
>    高级日历集成
*   numbered lists  编号列表
*   a half-dozen new themes (plus you can always CSS yourself natively)  
>    六个新主题（此外，您还可以使用原生 CSS 自行修改）
*   this crazy [calendar extension](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gvxDcTzeH7Y) complete with recurring tasks and other goodies  
>    这款功能强大的[日历扩展程序](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gvxDcTzeH7Y) ，包含重复任务和其他实用功能。
*   and dozens of patches and fixes  
>    以及数十个补丁和修复程序

They're slowly integrating all of WFx into the core app, which is awesome because WFx was where all the real power was.  
> 他们正在逐步将 WFx 的所有功能整合到核心应用程序中，这非常棒，因为 WFx 才是真正的强大功能所在。

[4:16](https://tanacommunity.slack.com/archives/C04K0V87F99/p1765311394917709?thread_ts=1765302104.541289&cid=C04K0V87F99)

But like the crazy thing is... if you asked me "what's new with Workflowy?" ~one year ago, I would have said, "Absolutely nothing for the past 10 years."  
> 但奇怪的是……如果你在一年前问我“Workflowy 有什么新变化？”，我会说，“过去 10 年里绝对没有任何变化。”

[4:18](https://tanacommunity.slack.com/archives/C04K0V87F99/p1765311486675809?thread_ts=1765302104.541289&cid=C04K0V87F99)

So I'm sure they've just injected their entire codebase (which I'm sure is dramatically smaller than Tana's since again, they're just managing one giant text file for folks) into Cursor and have been inspired and amplified by agentic coding workflows. Which is great, for the record!  
> 所以我确信他们只是把整个代码库（我敢肯定比 Tana 的代码库小得多，因为他们只是在管理一个巨大的文本文件）注入到了 Cursor 中，并且受到了智能编码工作流程的启发和增强。顺便说一句，这很棒！


## Message 5

Tana is ahead of Workflowy in the practice of adding things to the interface. Workflowy is ahead of Tana in the practice of subtracting things from the interface. Sometimes the best interface is less interface. ![:男士耸肩:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f937-200d-2642-fe0f@2x.png)
> Tana 在界面添加功能方面领先于 Workflowy。Workflowy 在界面精简方面领先于 Tana。有时，最好的界面就是简洁的界面。 ![:男士耸肩:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f937-200d-2642-fe0f@2x.png) I'm here because development of WF slowed to a crawl for many years, and because Tana introduced fields and structured data to the outliner format in a novel way. I tried Tana 3 years ago and slowly kept getting sucked deeper in. ![:笑哭:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f602@2x.png)
> 我来这里是因为 WF 的开发多年来进展缓慢，而且 Tana 以一种全新的方式将字段和结构化数据引入了大纲视图格式。三年前我尝试了 Tana，然后就慢慢地越陷越深了。 ![:笑哭:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f602@2x.png) But Tana is likely not my *forever*\-place, which I've said before. It's not local first, and my data is not encrypted such that I own the keys (the Tana team can read all of my data). Those two are important to me. But the local-first options (like Obsidian) are page-driven, and being an outliner-first is probably the most important thing to me (because of how much Workflowy taught me to love them). But for now, the latest offline additions have been awesome, and I just keep sensitive things away from the internet and cloud services.  
> 但正如我之前所说，Tana 可能不会是我*永远的*归宿。它并非以本地优先为核心，而且我的数据也没有加密，密钥不在我手中（Tana 团队可以读取我的所有数据）。这两点对我来说很重要。但以本地优先为核心的方案（比如 Obsidian）是页面驱动的，而以大纲视图为核心对我来说或许才是最重要的（因为 Workflowy 让我爱上了大纲视图）。不过就目前而言，它最新的离线功能非常棒，我只是把敏感信息放在远离互联网和云服务的地方。
